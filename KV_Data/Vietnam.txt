English Version===================================


Turning Again
The Falun turns again
Mountains ’n rivers changed
Heaven and earth purified
People regain kind thoughts
Traditions are revived
All truth about the cosmos is revealed
The Buddhas, Daos, and Deities among the crowd
Eradicate wicked thoughts to rectify people’s minds
All to fulfill my grand wish
December 1, 2009

Tái chuyển

Thiên địa tịnh hoá
